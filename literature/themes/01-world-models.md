---
title: "Market Simulation and World Models"
order: 1
---

Financial world models aim to simulate market dynamics at a granular level.
The MarS system \cite{li2024mars} introduces a market simulation engine
capable of generating realistic order-flow scenarios for strategy evaluation.

The Transformer architecture \cite{vaswani2017attention} underpins many
modern sequence models used in financial time-series prediction.
BERT-style pretraining \cite{devlin2019bert} has been adapted to financial
domains, enabling rich contextual representations of market events.
